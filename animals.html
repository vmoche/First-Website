Animals

Kenya is home to an extraordinary variety of wildlife, ranging from the majestic elephants and lions to the smallest insects and rare birds. Our sanctuary is dedicated to the protection and rehabilitation of these animals, many of which are endangered or threatened by habitat loss, poaching, and other human activities.
