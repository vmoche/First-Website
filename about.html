About Us

At Sanctuary Web, our mission is to protect and conserve wildlife in Kenya, a country rich in biodiversity. We are committed to providing a safe haven for endangered species and promoting environmental sustainability. Our sanctuary is home to over 25,000 animal species, including iconic large mammals like elephants, cheetahs, and giraffes, as well as countless plant and insect species.

We aim to educate the public about the importance of wildlife conservation through engaging programs, tours, and interactive exhibits. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that animals live in safe, natural environments and receive the care they need to thrive.
